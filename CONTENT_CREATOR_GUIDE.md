## 🎯 How to Pick Topics

### Research What People Search For

**Tools to use (free):**
1. **Google Suggest** - Start typing in Google, see autocomplete suggestions
2. **Google Search Console** - See what keywords bring traffic to similar sites
3. **Ubersuggest Free** - Check search volume
4. **Answer the Public** - See questions people ask about your topic

**Good topic questions:**
- "What's the best [furniture] for [situation]?"
- "How to [design/decorate/organize] [space]?"
- "[Number] best [product type] for [need]"
- "Budget [product type] that [benefit]"

**Examples:**
- ✓ "Best Modern Sofas for Small Living Rooms Under $1000"
- ✓ "How to Design a Minimalist Bedroom in 5 Steps"
- ✓ "7 Best Standing Desks for Work From Home"
- ✗ "Furniture" (too broad, harder to rank)
- ✗ "Beautiful Things" (not searchable)

### Topics with Money Potential

Focus on **"High Intent" topics** - things people search for when they're ready to buy:

| Topic Type | Search Intent | Money Potential |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| "Best [product] for [need]" | HIGH | ✓✓✓ Great |
| "How to [task]" | MEDIUM | ✓✓ Good |
| "[Product] review" | HIGH | ✓✓✓ Great |
| "[Brand] vs [brand]" | HIGH | ✓✓✓ Great |
| "[Product] guide" | MEDIUM | ✓✓ Good |
| "[Style] home ideas" | LOW | ✓ Fair |

**Best topics combine:**
1. High search volume (people actually search for it)
2. Commercial intent (they might buy)
3. You have expertise/passion (you'll write better)

## ✍️ Writing Great Content

### Structure That Converts

**Opening Hook (100 words)**
- Start with a relatable problem
- Make promise of solution
- Capture reader interest

Example:
> "Your living room feels cramped. You want a sofa that's stylish AND functional, but everything under $1000 looks cheap. What if I told you there are sofas at half that price that look $5000? I've tested dozens of sofas in small spaces, and I'm sharing my top 7 that actually work."

**Table of Contents (optional)**
- Helps readers find what they need
- Improves SEO
- Looks professional

**Main Content (1000-2000 words)**
- Use headers to break up text (H2, H3)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Mix text with images/products
- Use bullets and numbered lists

**Conclusion (150 words)**
- Summarize key points
- Call to action (read more, explore products, etc.)

### Writing Tips for Web

**1. Write for Skimming**
Most readers don't read every word. They scan. So:
- Use bold text for key phrases
- Use short paragraphs
- Use lots of white space
- Use headers frequently

**2. Write in Active Voice**
- ✓ "This sofa offers amazing comfort" 
- ✗ "Amazing comfort is offered by this sofa"

**3. Use Power Words**
- Best, proven, ultimate, essential, expert
- Easy, simple, quick, effortless
- Revolutionary, innovative, cutting-edge
- Cozy, elegant, stunning, beautiful

**4. Be Specific (Not Vague)**
- ✓ "Costs $299-399 depending on fabric"
- ✗ "Affordable"

- ✓ "Seats 4 people comfortably"
- ✗ "Large sofa"

- ✓ "Microfiber fabric resists stains"
- ✗ "Durable material"

**5. Show Authority**
- "I tested this for 6 months"
- "As an interior designer with 10 years experience"
- "According to a 2025 study..."
- "I own this product and here's what happened"

### Content Structure Example

```
# 7 Best Modern Sofas for Small Living Rooms (Under $1000)

## Introduction [100 words]
Problem + Promise + Hook

## What Makes a Good Small Living Room Sofa [200 words]

Durability
Comfort

[Same structure]

[Continue for #3-7]

## FAQs [200 words]
What size sofa fits a small room?
What fabric is most durable?
Etc.

## Conclusion [150 words]
Summary
Final recommendation
Call to action

Total: ~2000 words
```

## 💰 Affiliate Strategy

### 1. Product Research

Before writing about a product:
- [ ] Google it + read 5 reviews
- [ ] Check price on multiple retailers
- [ ] Test if affiliate link works
- [ ] Make sure you'd actually recommend it
- [ ] Check competitor posts about it

### 2. Product Positioning

**Position products throughout the post:**
1. **Early mention** (text block) - "As I'll show you..."
2. **Detailed review** (product embed) - Full card with link
3. **Comparison** (comparison table) - vs competitors
4. **Final recommendation** (product embed) - "My top pick"

**Example placement:**
```
Text Block: "The best affordable sofas combine..."
Product Embed: "IKEA Kivik - Great Budget Option"
Text Block: "What makes this work..."
Comparison Table: "Small Sofas Compared"
Product Embed: "Wayfair Article - Best All-Around"
Text Block: "Final thoughts..."
Product Embed: "Crate & Barrel Sofa - My Top Pick"
```

### 3. Honest Recommendations

**Things that build trust:**
- ✓ "I own this product" (use present tense)
- ✓ "This isn't perfect for X reason, but..."
- ✓ "Better option if you need Y"
- ✓ Include pros AND cons
- ✓ Real customer quotes

**Things that destroy trust:**
- ✗ Recommend EVERYTHING (no credibility)
- ✗ Hide that it's an affiliate link
- ✗ Make up features
- ✗ Ignore real problems

### 4. Commission Optimization

**Know your numbers:**
- Which products have highest commissions?
- Which posts get most traffic?
- Which products get most clicks?

**Write more about high-earning content:**
- If "Best Sofas" gets 1000 clicks/month
- And gets 50 affiliate clicks
- That's 5% conversion (great!)
- Write more sofa content

**Example strategy:**
- Write post about product with 10% commission
- Drive traffic with SEO
- If 100 people click, you earn 10 commissions
- Average order $500 = $5000 revenue for you

## 🔍 SEO (Getting Found in Google)

### The SEO Fundamentals

Google ranks content that:
1. **Solves the reader's problem** (main)
2. **Matches search intent** (what they searched for)
3. **Is well-written and clear** (readability)
4. **Has relevant links** (authority)
5. **Loads fast** (technical)

### Keyword Strategy

**1. Find Your Target Keyword**
Choose ONE primary keyword per post:
- "Best Modern Sofas for Small Rooms"
- "How to Organize a Small Bedroom Closet"
- "Budget Friendly Dining Room Sets"

**2. Use Keyword in Key Places**
- Title (first 60 characters)
- First sentence
- One main header
- Natural mentions throughout

**3. Don't Keyword Stuff**
- ✓ "Modern sofas for small rooms are trending"
- ✗ "Modern sofas small rooms sofas modern modern"

### Backlinks (Other Sites Linking to You)

How to earn backlinks:
- [ ] Guest post on interior design blogs
- [ ] Get mentioned in design roundups
- [ ] Build relationships with other creators
- [ ] Create content so good people want to link it
- [ ] Submit to relevant directories

### Title & Description Strategy

Your title appears in Google search results:

**Good titles:**
- Clear keyword at start
- Benefit-focused
- Curiosity-inducing
- 50-60 characters

Examples:
- "7 Best Small Space Sofas Under $1000 [2026]"
- "Modern Home Decor on a Budget: 23 Ideas"
- "Minimalist Bedroom Ideas That Actually Feel Cozy"

**Meta descriptions** (summary under title in Google):
- Should make reader want to click
- Include keyword naturally
- 150-160 characters
- Call to action or benefit

Example:
"Discover 7 best sofas for small living rooms. Quality seating under $1000 that looks expensive. Expert picks + styling tips."

## 📸 Working with Images

### Image Best Practices

**For cover images:**
- Size: 1200x600 pixels (2:1 ratio)
- Quality: High resolution, beautiful
- Show the product or styled space
- Use consistent style across posts

**For body images:**
- Use throughout content (every 300 words)
- Label with alt text (for accessibility)
- Mix lifestyle photos with product close-ups
- Compress for speed (but keep quality)

**Image sources:**
- **Free:** Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay
- **Licensed:** Shutterstock, Adobe Stock
- **Your own:** Best! Shows authenticity
- **Product photos:** Manufacturer images are usually okay

## 📊 Tracking Your Success

### Metrics to Monitor

**Traffic Metrics:**
- Page views (is anyone reading?)
- Time on page (are they engaged?)
- Bounce rate (low = good, they're reading)

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Click-through rate (% who click affiliate links)
- Conversion rate (% who buy after clicking)
- Average order value (how much did they spend)

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Clicks per post
- Commission per post
- Revenue trend (going up or down?)

### Using Data to Improve

If a post gets traffic but no clicks:
- Add more product recommendations
- Move product embeds earlier
- Improve product descriptions

If a post gets no traffic:
- Check if keyword is too competitive
- Rewrite title for better clarity
- Build backlinks to it
- Update content (fresher = better)

## 🎨 Writing in Different Styles

### Style 1: Listicle (Most Popular)

"7 Best [Product] for [Situation]"

**Structure:**
- Intro with why you chose these
- #1 - Most important/best overall
- #2 - Best budget option
- #3 - Best luxury option
- #4-7 - Various others
- Conclusion with final recommendation

**Why it works:** Clear, scannable, searchable

---

### Style 2: How-To Guide

"How to [Accomplish Task]"

**Structure:**
- Problem identification
- Materials/tools needed
- Step-by-step instructions
- Troubleshooting
- Tips for success

**Example:**
"How to Design a Minimalist Living Room in 5 Steps"
- Step 1: Declutter ruthlessly
- Step 2: Choose a color palette
- Step 3: Select key furniture pieces
- Step 4: Add functional storage
- Step 5: Style with intention

---

### Style 3: Comparison

"[Product A] vs [Product B]"

**Structure:**
- Intro: Why compare these
- Head-to-head comparison table
- Detailed comparison for each feature
- Best for each use case
- Conclusion with recommendation

**Example:**
"IKEA Billy Bookcase vs Crate & Barrel Belmont Shelf"
- Price comparison
- Style comparison
- Durability comparison
- Who should buy each

---

### Style 4: Problem/Solution

"[Problem] - [Solution]"

**Structure:**
- Detailed problem statement
- Why this problem exists
- Solution 1 + why
- Solution 2 + why
- Solution 3 + why
- Conclusion with best approach

**Example:**
"Small Living Room Too Dark - 7 Ways to Add Light"

## ✅ Pre-Publication Checklist

Before hitting publish:

**Content Quality:**
- [ ] Title is compelling and includes keyword
- [ ] Article is 1000+ words
- [ ] Has multiple sections (H2, H3 headers)
- [ ] Has 2+ images
- [ ] Grammar/spelling checked (use Grammarly)
- [ ] Tone is consistent
- [ ] Flows naturally (read it out loud)

**SEO:**
- [ ] Meta title filled in (50-60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description filled in (150-160 chars)
- [ ] Keyword appears in title and first paragraph
- [ ] Keywords feel natural (not stuffed)
- [ ] Internal links to 1-3 other posts

**Affiliate:**
- [ ] All affiliate links are correct
- [ ] Links open in new tab
- [ ] At least 3 product recommendations
- [ ] Products positioned throughout (not all at end)
- [ ] Category selected
- [ ] Tags added (3-5 relevant)

**Technical:**
- [ ] Cover image is high quality (1200x600)
- [ ] All images have alt text
- [ ] Blocks are formatted correctly
- [ ] No broken links
- [ ] Preview looks good on mobile

**Final Review:**
- [ ] Re-read for clarity
- [ ] Check for typos
- [ ] Ask: "Would I recommend this to a friend?"

## 🚀 Publishing Workflow

1. **Research** (1-2 hours)
   - Find keyword
   - Research competitors
   - Gather product info

2. **Outline** (30 min)
   - Write main sections
   - Plan product placements
   - Note keywords

3. **Write** (2-4 hours)
   - First draft
   - Add images
   - Fill in product embeds

4. **Edit** (1 hour)
   - Grammar check
   - Clarity review
   - Keyword verification

5. **SEO Review** (30 min)
   - Add meta title/description
   - Check keyword placement
   - Internal link check

6. **Final Preview** (30 min)
   - Mobile view check
   - Link check
   - Image check

7. **Publish**
   - Click publish
   - Share on social (optional)
   - Email subscribers (optional)

## 🤝 Final Tips for Success

**1. Write What You Know**
Best content comes from real expertise and passion. If you love design, it shows.

**2. Solve Real Problems**
Don't write about what you think people want to read. Write what they actually search for.

**3. Be Honest**
Recommend products you'd actually use. Your reputation is your biggest asset.

**4. Consistency Matters**
Regular publishing (even 2x/month) beats sporadic posts.

**5. Update Old Content**
Your best posts will be 6-12 months old. Update them with new products and refresh SEO.

**6. Track Everything**
Know which posts make money. Double down on what works.

**7. Have Fun**
You'll write better when you enjoy the topic. Your enthusiasm is contagious.
